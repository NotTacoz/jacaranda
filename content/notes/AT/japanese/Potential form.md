## What
- Expresses ability or possibility to do action described by verb.
- e.g.
	- can eat 食べられる
	- can drink 飲める
	- can swim 泳げる
## Other Uses
### Asking for Permission
- "can" in english can be used for permission. e.g. "can i go to the bathroom?"
