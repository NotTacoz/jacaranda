## how do i divide? part 2
- $\frac{a+bi}{c+di} = \frac{a+bi}{c+di} \times \frac{c-di}{c-di}=\frac{(a+bi)(c-di)}{(c+di)(c-di)}=\frac{(a+bi)(c-di)}{c^2+d^2}$ and then simplify :D
