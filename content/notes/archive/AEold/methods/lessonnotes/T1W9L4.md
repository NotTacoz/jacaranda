title: "T1W9L4: inverse parabola"
---

- basic inverse parabola: $y^2=x$
  - properties and features
    - vertex at (0,0)
    - as y -> inf, x -> inf
    - as y -> -inf, x -> -inf
    - line of symmetry at y=0
    - domain: x subset R, x >\_ 0
- $y=a(x-h)^2+k$
- $y-k=a(x-h)^2$
- $\frac{y-k}{a}=(x-h)^2$
- $(y-k)^2=a^2(x-h)$
