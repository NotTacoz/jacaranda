# Distances and Midpoints (RECAP)
- The distance and midpoint between point A (x1, y1) and point B (x2, y2) can be found as:
Distance: $$AB = \sqrt{(y_2-y_1)^2+(x_2-x_1)^2}$$
Midpoint: $$M_{AB}=(\frac{x_1+x_2}{2},\frac{y_1+y_2}{2})$$
